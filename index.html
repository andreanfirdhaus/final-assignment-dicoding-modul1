Frequently Asked Question

If you are a new CarePet client, you may have some questions about how it works.

  • Do You Offer Care For More Than Dogs & Cats?

    Yes, at CarePet, we offer comprehensive care services for a variety of pets beyond dogs and cats. Our dedicated team is experienced in providing specialized care for small mammals, birds, and other exotic companions.

  • Is CarePet insured and bonded?

    Absolutely. CarePet is fully insured and bonded. We prioritize the safety and security of your beloved pets, and our comprehensive insurance coverage provides an additional layer of protection and peace of mind.

  • How Do I Request My Pet’s Care Services?

    Requesting care services for your pet at CarePet is a simple process. Contact us through our website or give us a call to initiate the conversation. Schedule a consultation, discuss your pet's needs, and work with our team to create a personalized care plan. Booking services is then easy and tailored to your pet's specific requirements.

  • Are There any Cancellation fees?

    At our facility, we understand that plans may change, and flexibility is essential. We strive to make our cancellation policy fair and reasonable, prioritizing open communication and understanding between our clients and our team.
